About

Visual interaction design is where my passion lies. While attending university, I focused on my degree program, game programming, and development, but I fell in love with UI. I love doing research into the whys and whats of how people felt about certain things and how to make their needs come to life. After finishing my degree, I came to realize that I was inadvertently always working on UI and doing best design practices.

Before working on assignments, I would do research into what people liked the most and what they struggled with, focusing on colors and actions. I would then outline (wireframe) how I wanted my applications and games to look and feel to my targeted audience. I would annotate within the programs what was working and what needed to be fixed, while adding what additional items I would like to include, such as further interactivity or better clarification of button functionality. At the end of my degree program, I worked as the UI/UX engineer and designer for my gaming group and solidified my love for all things UI.
